Lore

In a far away spot in the cosmos lived two civilizations in a solar system orbiting around two Suns.

One was the nation of humans. A nation of war, fighting for their right to exist for thousands of years. Over the past millennia the kingdom was ruled by Princess Gravitas Nexis overseeing the progress of the people, leading them on their ways. During her reign the nation became stronger and more technologically advanced than ever before.

The other civilizations was the nation of aliens. The enemy of local humans', obsessed with them in the worst way, from the moment they first met. The evil organisms' aim was to enslave people, and use them for anything they wished, accompanied by cruel experiments and torture, what they called "befriending". This twisted goal became the entire point of the aliens' existence. Out of lots of items repeated in human cultures flower motifs were the aliens' favorite, that they incorporated in all of theirs destructive inventions.

Wars between the two lasted for thousands of years.

During an expedition, one of alien special forces managed to obtain a peculiar creature, the body of which was composed of a special material that could change its shape after strong energy impacts. The aliens tried countless methods to shape the creature in the exact way they wanted, some of which succeeded, some of which did not. Being conscious, it went thought a lot of additional training, main focus of what was to teach the goal of the war with humans and for what to fight. But the creature didn't share their views, but on the contrary - it sided with the humans, which dissatisfied aliens to no end. The leader of the research conducted a lot of cruel experiments on the creature, causing it great pain. After a while, the "mad scientist" discarded the test subject into the open space.

The creature remained alive, alone, with nothing left to do and no fear of death. With a lot of effort it has managed to get to the bay of the humans' space station. The creature passed a single gate while a small space ship was unloaded and fueled. The gate closed immediately as the base's security system reacted to the intrusion.

The princess, having the highest authority, learned about it as well, and set out to personally meet the intruder lifeform. The cat-like creature was left in awe, having only seen Gravitas Nexis in its textbooks before. The princess looked cold and serious befitting her status.

Suddenly, the air froze, and shiver went down both of their the spines. The creature impaled the princess through her heart with a large spear. Apparently, the little alien was embedded with a sophisticated attacking system, launching automatically at the presence of any human organism. The princess attempted to defend herself with a large sword she quickly forged out of thin air, but the spear shattered it to pieces, piercing the princess in the process. A large flower bloomed from the victim, marking her defeat.

Because of the powerful energy impact launching from the creature - it took the shape of the princess herself, becoming her perfect copy.

The door behind the creature opened. Two soldiers charged in to catch the intruder, but the alien blew past them without thinking. One of them got to helping the wounded princess, while his partner ran to catch the impostor. The "new" Princess Gravitas Nexis found the same ship from before, quickly jumped in, and flew away.

The shapeshifter found human equipment, weapons and a data bank, with the ship's cruising path as well as information and images featuring itself in its original form, living among humans. After reading more into the data, it found out that it was a result of the princess' research and development activities all along. The memories of which were gone, just as the opportunity to mend what has been done. The new pilot's navigation skills were subpar, to say the least, so it couldn't make its way back to the base to save the face. So it continued its aimless voyage having little hope, contemplating, and thinking of what could fill the void caused by the recent tragedy.

It has dressed up in the armor it found to remind itself of what it did less, and chose to call itself simply Nexis, to honor the legacy of its creator. After a lot of time of travel the shapeshifter accidentally encountered the Earth by picking up signals from it. It surveyed where it would be safest to land and, enabling masking, landed in a forest in Ukraine. Nexis left the ship, and went to explore where the journey took her, marking the beginning of her life on Earth.
